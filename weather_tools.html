Weather Maps

Visualise the atmosphere with clarity — maps built from robust reanalysis and observation data.

Our Weather Maps tools transform raw atmospheric data, such as surface air temperature (T2m), mean sea level pressure (MSLP), wind, total cloud cover, and total precipitation; into clear, visual maps. These maps provide a spatial representation of the atmosphere, making it easier to understand weather patterns, identify trends, and support decision-making in fields like aviation, renewable energy, agriculture, and environmental monitoring.
